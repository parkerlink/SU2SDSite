What is SU2SD?

The #StepUp2ScaleDown campaign is designed to allow everyone, especially students and faculty in urban settings to stay fit and to become healthier by abstaining from escalators and elevators and instead by using the stairs. Teachers at every level can implement a #StepUp2ScaleDown challenge without loosing precious class time. It can be encouraged before and/or after class, or during recess. The increased oxygenation and blood flow to brain and the large working muscles that results will also increase the alertness and mental readiness of students in the classroom. Teachers can also assign a #StepUp2ScaleDown project in which steps are counted and goals for individuals or groups are achieved. Weekly data can add to the fun and help denote progress. The simplicity and cost-free availability of a #StepUp2ScaleDown will also allow teachers to conduct such challenges without complication or constraint. Students who participate will develop a higher awareness about how physical mobility choices can work to counter the sedentary lifestyle that plaques the health of so many in our contemporary society.
